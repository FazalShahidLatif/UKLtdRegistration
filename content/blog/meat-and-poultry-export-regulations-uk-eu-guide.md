# Navigating UK and EU Meat Export Regulations: The UK Ltd Advantage

Exporting **meat** and poultry products to the **UK and European markets** requires navigating some of the world's most stringent health and safety regulations. For a meat processor in South Asia, South America, or Africa, the barrier to entry can seem insurmountable without a local presence.

A **UK based** limited company acts as your regulatory and financial bridge to these high-value markets.

## Simplifying Compliance via a UK Hub
When you **open a company in the uk**, you create a legal entity that can hold the necessary import licenses and health certifications required by the Food Standards Agency (FSA) and EU authorities.

-   **Landed Pricing**: A UK entity can be VAT-registered, allowing you to provide "landed" pricing to European wholesalers, removing the tax headache for your buyers.
-   **Local Responsibility**: Having a **person with significant control** (PSC) and a **registered office address** in the UK provides the accountability that major food retailers demand from their suppliers.

## Essential SIC Codes for Meat Exporters
To ensure your **company details** correctly reflect your trade, use these SIC codes:
-   **46320**: Wholesale of **meat** and meat products.
-   **10110**: Processing and preserving of meat.

These codes are vital when you apply to **open a uk business bank account**, as they categorize your risk profile for the bank's compliance team.
