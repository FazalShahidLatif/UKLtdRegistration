# Sialkot to the World: Exporting Safety Wear & Sports Goods using a UK Entity

Sialkot is the beating heart of Pakistan’s manufacturing sector, producing everything from FIFA World Cup footballs to precision surgical instruments and **high-performance safety wear**. However, for many Sialkot-based SMEs, the jump to the **USA and European markets** can feel daunting due to the lack of a "local" presence in those regions.

By establishing a **UK based** limited company, Sialkot manufacturers can transform their export business into a global brand.

## The "London Office" Advantage for Sialkot Brands
When a buyer in Germany or the USA looks for **safety wear** or **leather fashion wear**, they prioritize reliability. Having a **registered office address** in London, provided by your UK company, gives your brand an immediate aura of international quality and compliance.

### Leveraging UK Banking for Global Payments
One of the biggest pain points for Sialkot exporters is receiving payments from international clients without high fees or long delays. A **UK business bank account** (via partners like Wise or Tide) allows you to:
- Receive payments in USD from US buyers via ACH.
- Receive EUR from European clients via SEPA.
- Convert these into PKR at competitive market rates to bring back to Pakistan.

## Understanding the SIC Code 15110 & 14190
For exporters of **leather wear** and **safety wear**, choosing the right Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) code is vital for Companies House compliance:
- **15110**: Tanning and dressing of leather; dressing and dyeing of fur.
- **14190**: Manufacture of other wearing apparel and accessories.

Using these codes correctly ensures that your **company details** accurately reflect your business activities to international banks and tax authorities.

## Exploring European Markets
With a UK company, you can store stock in UK warehouses and ship to the EU with much more flexibility than shipping individual small orders from Pakistan. This "hub and spoke" model is how the most successful Sialkot exporters are scaling in 2026.
