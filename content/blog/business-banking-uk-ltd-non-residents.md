## The Bank Gap: Why Old Banks Say No to Remote Bosses

Old UK banks (Barclays, HSBC, Lloyds) are very hard to use in 2026. The reasons are mostly due to strict risk rules:

*   **Face to Face Needs:** Most old banks still want a face to face chat at a UK branch for an ID check.
*   **Proof of UK Home:** They ask the boss to show a UK power bill or a UK tax form.
*   **Strict Risk Rules:** Under 2026 anti-crime rules, old banks get huge fines for bad checks on foreign accounts. So, they just say "no".

**Win Rate for Remote Bosses:** ~5% to 10%. Unless you live in the UK or have a huge cash pile (over £250k), do not apply to old banks.

## Tech Needs: ID Check and Proof of Home

Under the **2026 UK state rules**, you can not check your own ID. Every boss must be checked.

### 1. Photo ID Check
You must use a phone app from us to scan your passport. You also take a live selfie video. This check links to your state file. We can share it with banks to speed up your setup.

### 2. Proof of Home Address
Banks need a form dated within the last 3 months. Good forms include:
*   Power, Water, or Gas bills.
*   Bank statements. Digital PDFs are often fine.
*   Tax forms from your local town hall.

## Questions and Answers (FAQs)

### 1. Can I open a UK bank from afar?
Yes. 100% of the banks we like (Wise, Revolut, Mercury) offer a full web setup path.

### 2. Which bank is best for Amazon FBA?
Wise and Payoneer are the best. They link perfectly with Amazon. You get cash in local types (USD/GBP/EUR) with low fees.

### 3. Do I need a UK phone line for codes?
Having a UK virtual line (via apps like Skype) is highly smart. It looks pro and ensures you get SMS text codes with no fail.

### 4. How long does the check take?
*   **Wise:** 1 to 2 days.
*   **Revolut:** 2 to 7 work days.
*   **Mercury:** 3 to 5 work days.

### 5. What are the base cash needs?
Most digital banks have a zero base cash need. Some may ask for a small start drop (like £20) to turn on your debit card.

### 6. Can I get USD straight into my UK bank?
Yes, if you use a multi-cash bank like Wise or Mercury. They give you a US Route Number that feeds right into your UK firm pile.

### 7. What is an ACSP?
It is an Approved firm to verify IDs. Banks trust checked founders much more. This leads to higher win rates.

### 8. Will the bank check my web page?
Yes. Banks always check your firm's web page. It must look clean and show what you sell.

### 9. Can I use a personal bank for my firm?
No. UK law says a Limited firm is its own legal person. It must have its own business bank account.

### 10. Does a bad credit score stop me?
Usually no. If you use Wise or Revolut, they do not run a hard credit check in your home country.

### 11. Can the bank freeze my cash?
Yes, if they suspect bad acts. Always keep clean records and sales proof. This keeps your account safe.
