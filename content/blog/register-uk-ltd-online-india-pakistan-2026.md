## Tax and Compliance: Pakistan-UK Dual Treaty

Pakistan and the United Kingdom have a **Dual Taxation Treaty**. This is critical for **Pakistan to UK company** setups.
*   **Key Advantage**: You generally only pay corporation tax in the UK on your UK profits. 
*   **Legal Requirements**: Ensure you report your **company details** to both HMRC and potentially the **State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)** if you are remitting large sums of capital back home.
