## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Airwallex Setup for Non-Residents

Airwallex actively supports non-resident UK LTD owners, but their compliance onboarding is highly detail-oriented. Follow this checklist to ensure a swift, successful approval:

### 1. Corporate Identity Match
Airwallex's system automatically queries the **Companies House register**. Your application parameters must align perfectly:
*   **Legal Structure:** The company name and registration number must match your official Certificate of Incorporation.
*   **Active SIC Codes:** Ensure your company’s SIC code reflects your actual business sector (e.g. `62012` for business software development or `47910` for retail).

### 2. Required Onboarding Documentation
*   **Corporate Documents:**
    *   UK Certificate of Incorporation.
    *   Shareholder registry detailing all Persons with Significant Control (PSCs holding 25%+).
    *   Proof of active trading address (Airwallex accepts virtual office leases if backed by a valid, physical UK Registered Office address - PO Boxes are strictly banned in 2026).
*   **Director & UBO Verification:**
    *   Valid biometric passport scans for all active directors.
    *   Residential proof of address for directors (utility bill or bank statement dated within the last 3 months).

### 3. The Digital Footprint Check (Crucial)
Airwallex will manually inspect your business's online presence to verify legitimacy.
*   You must provide an active, secure website (`https://`).
*   The website must display a clear description of your products or services, transparent pricing, refund policies, and a contact email/address.
*   *Note for Exporters:* If you are exporting physical goods from regions like South Asia (India/Pakistan) or China, provide active supplier agreements or custom clearance bills to speed up approval.
