3.  **Secure Payments**: Use your **UK business bank account** to manage multi-currency contracts, ensuring that your high-ticket technology payments are handled securely and transparently.

## Strategic SIC Codes for Tech Exporters
To ensure your **company registration** is compliant for technology tenders, use:
-   **46520**: Wholesale of electronic and telecommunications equipment and parts.
-   **62090**: Other information technology and computer service activities.

Selecting the right code is vital for your **company details** and for the approval of your **UK business bank account**.
