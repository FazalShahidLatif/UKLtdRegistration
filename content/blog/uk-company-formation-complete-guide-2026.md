## 🏗️ Phase 1: Structuring for Success

### 1. Choosing Your Legal Blueprint
The **Private Limited Company (Ltd)** remains the vehicle of choice for 95% of businesses. It provides:
- **Limited Liability**: Protection of your personal assets.
- **Tax Efficiency**: Eligibility for Small Profits Relief and various R&D tax credits.
- **Investment Readiness**: Structured for share issuance and venture capital.

### 2. Standard Industrial Classification (SIC) Accuracy
Choosing the correct SIC code (Standard Industrial Classification) is now a prerequisite for banking approval. The 2026 registry uses AI to flag "Incongruent Classifications," so accuracy from Day 1 is critical.
