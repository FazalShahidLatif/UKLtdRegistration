# Bangladesh's Ready-Made Garment (RMG) Revolution: Boosting Exports via UK Strategic Hubs

Bangladesh’s **Ready-Made Garment (RMG)** sector is a global powerhouse, feeding the inventory of the world’s largest fashion retailers. However, for many factory owners in Dhaka and Gazipur, the profit margins are often squeezed by intermediaries and complex cross-border logistics.

By establishing a **UK based** limited company, RMG manufacturers can take direct control of their exports and capture higher value from the **USA and European markets**.

## Why a UK Presence Matters for RMG
In the fast-moving fashion industry, proximity and trust are key. When you **open a company in the uk**, you gain a strategic base that allows you to:
-   **Invoice Locally**: Many European retailers prefer to deal with a UK-registered entity due to simpler tax and legal frameworks.
-   **Register for VAT**: As a UK entity, you can manage your import VAT and offer "landed" pricing to your European customers, making your garments more competitive.
-   **Build a Brand**: Instead of just being a "white-label" manufacturer, use your London **registered office address** to launch your own boutique fashion label for the western market.

## Strategic SIC Codes for RMG Exporters
To correctly classify your **textile** and apparel business, you should use:
-   **14131**: Manufacture of other men's outerwear.
-   **14132**: Manufacture of other women's outerwear.
-   **46420**: Wholesale of clothing and footwear.

These codes are essential for your **company registration** and are highly recognized by **high street banks** when you apply for a **UK business bank account**.

### Financial Efficiency and Forex
By collecting payments in GBP and EUR through your UK hub, you can hedge against currency fluctuations in the Bangladeshi Taka (BDT). Repatriating these earnings as export revenue helps boost Bangladesh's foreign exchange reserves and supports the national economy.
