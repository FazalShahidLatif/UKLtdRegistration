## Strategic SIC Codes for Car Exporters
To ensure your **company details** are compliant for the automotive trade, use:
-   **45112**: Sales of used cars and light motor vehicles.
-   **46140**: Agents involved in the sale of machinery, industrial equipment, ships and aircraft.

Selecting the correct code is vital for your **UK business bank account** approval, as the automotive trade is often heavily scrutinized for AML compliance.
