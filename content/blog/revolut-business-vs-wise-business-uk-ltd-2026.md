# Revolut Business vs Wise Business UK Ltd: The Ultimate 2026 Comparison

Starting a UK company is easy. However, opening a bank account is hard. Big high-street banks will often say no to international founders. They want you to live in the UK. They also want to see you in person. 

This is where digital banks help. Electronic Money Institutions (EMIs) like **Wise Business** and **Revolut Business** solve this problem. In 2026, they use modern tech to verify your identity. You can open an account from your phone. You do not need to visit the UK.

But which one is best for your business? This simple guide compares their fees, features, and approval rates.

> [!NOTE]
> **What is an EMI?**
> Neither Wise nor Revolut is a traditional bank. They are Electronic Money Institutions. The UK Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) regulates them to keep your money safe.

## 💸 Deep Dive: Foreign Exchange & Transfer Mechanics

For global exporters—especially dropshippers, SaaS agencies, and e-commerce sellers—foreign exchange (FX) rates will represent the largest banking cost.

### Wise Business: The Mid-Market Standard
Wise does not add hidden markups to the exchange rate. When you convert GBP to USD or EUR to GBP, Wise uses the exact mid-market rate (the real rate you see on Google). 

Instead of hiding profit in a marked-up exchange rate, Wise charges a highly transparent, variable fee that scales with volume. Typically, this ranges between **0.33% and 0.45%** of the transaction amount.
*   *Example:* If you transfer £10,000 to USD, you pay a transparent fee of roughly £38, and your funds are converted at the pure interbank rate.

### Revolut Business: Tiered Limits & Weekend Margins
Revolut uses a different methodology. They offer completely fee-free currency exchange up to a certain threshold depending on your monthly subscription:
*   **Free Plan:** £1,000 fee-free exchange per month (0.6% markup thereafter).
*   **Grow Plan (£19/mo):** £10,000 fee-free exchange per month.
*   **Scale Plan (£79/mo):** £50,000 fee-free exchange per month.

> [!WARNING]
> **The Weekend Markup Trap:** Revolut applies a strict **0.6% to 1.0% markup** on all currency exchanges conducted on Saturdays and Sundays (when foreign exchange markets are closed). For high-volume businesses, converting funds over the weekend on Revolut can quickly become extremely expensive.

## 💳 Expense Management & Corporate Cards

If your business has multiple team members, virtual assistants, or media buyers running Facebook and Google Ads, card issuing and spending controls are paramount.

### Wise Business Cards
Wise provides highly durable corporate Mastercards. You can order physical cards for team members and generate unlimited digital virtual cards.
*   **Spend Controls:** You can set basic daily or monthly limits per card.
*   **Atmosphere:** Ideal for simple business travel and standard software subscriptions.

### Revolut Business Cards (The Industry Leader)
Revolut shines exceptionally bright in this category. Their expense management suite is on par with specialized corporate card platforms like Brex or Ramp.
*   **Granular Controls:** Assign cards with custom daily, weekly, or category-specific limits (e.g. restrict a card to *Advertising* or *Travel* only).
*   **Auto-Receipt Matching:** Team members take a photo of their receipt via the Revolut mobile app, and the system automatically matches it to the transaction, syncing directly with your ledger.
*   **Virtual Cards:** Instantly generate disposable virtual cards that auto-delete after a single transaction, providing supreme security against online software subscription fraud.

## 🔒 2026 KYC & Biometric Identity Verification

Under the **2026 Companies House ECCTA regulations**, both Wise and Revolut have updated their onboarding to support biometric verification for international directors.

### Wise Onboarding Speed
*   **Residency:** Supports non-residents in 170+ countries.
*   **Process:** Fully digital. You scan your biometric passport and perform a 3D "liveness check" through your smartphone camera.
*   **Timeline:** Most accounts are approved within **24 to 48 hours** if your company documentation matches the Companies House register.

### Revolut Onboarding Speed
*   **Residency:** Generally strict on supported countries. Revolut requires the parent company to be registered in an active region (US, UK, EEA, Switzerland, etc.).
*   **Process:** Requires comprehensive proof of company trading activity. You must show active contracts, a professional website, or supplier invoices to be approved.
*   **Timeline:** Verification usually takes **3 to 7 business days** due to manually conducted compliance audits.

## ⚖️ Strategic Verdict: Which Should You Choose?

### Choose Wise Business if:
1.  **You are a Solo Entrepreneur or non-resident** looking for the easiest, absolute fastest setup with zero ongoing monthly maintenance fees.
2.  **Your primary cost is high-volume foreign exchange** (e.g. dropshippers sourcing from China in USD and selling to UK clients in GBP).
3.  **You reside in South Asia (Pakistan, India, Bangladesh) or the Gulf**—Wise has historically higher remote approval rates for these specific non-resident pathways.

### Choose Revolut Business if:
1.  **You run an agency or scaling startup** with multiple team members who require individual expense cards and advanced receipt collection tracking.
2.  **You require robust corporate card limits** for running high-ticket digital ad campaigns (Facebook, Google, TikTok Ads).
3.  **You can easily justify a paid tier (£19/mo or £79/mo)** to unlock massive fee-free exchange limits and priority 24/7 client support.
