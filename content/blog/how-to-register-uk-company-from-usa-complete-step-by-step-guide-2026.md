# How to Register a UK Company from the USA: Step-by-Step

## The Bridge Across the Atlantic

For US-based entrepreneurs, the UK represents the most logical first step for international expansion. With a shared language, similar legal foundations, and a favorable tax treaty, the UK is a "home away from home" for American business. 

Starting a **business in the UK** allows you to leverage a prestigious **limited company** structure while **living in the UK** or staying **anywhere in the world**.

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Structure (Hint: It’s likely a Private Limited Company)

Most US entrepreneurs choose a **Private Limited Company (Ltd)**. It is the gold standard for **company formation** and is recognized by every **uk business bank**. Unlike the US, where "LLCs" are common, the UK **limited company** provides a distinct legal personality that is globally respected.

---

## Step 2: The "Registered Office Address" Requirement

Under UK law, every **company in the uk** must have a physical **registered office address**. This is where **Companies House** and HMRC will send official correspondence. You cannot use a US address for this **legal requirement**.

**The Solution**: Use a professional service for your **company registration**. We provide a prestigious London or Edinburgh address to keep your US residential address off the public record.

---

## Step 3: Appoint Directors and Shareholders

To **register a uk** entity, you will need to appoint at least one **director** and one **shareholder**.
*   **Can they be the same person?** Yes.
*   **Do they need to be UK residents?** No. Most of our clients are non-**uk residents**.
*   **Significant Control (PSC)**: In 2026, you must declare who has **significant control** over the company to maintain transparency.
*   **Identity Verification**: You will need to provide your **company details** and verify your identity to comply with current AML regulations.

---

## Step 4: Register for Corporation Tax & VAT

Once your **company formation** is complete, you must **register for corporation tax**. 
*   **VAT registration**: If your turnover exceeds £90,000, you must **register for vat**. Many US companies choose to register voluntarily to claim back business expenses.

---

## Step 5: Incorporation and Digital Documents

Once we submit your application, **Companies House** usually approves it within **24 hours**. You will receive your digital certificate of incorporation and share certificates via email.

---

## Step 6: Opening a UK Business Bank Account from the USA

This is the most critical step for US founders. To operate a **business in the uk**, you need a dedicated **uk business bank** account. 
*   **Fintech Solutions**: We recommend **Wise Business** or **Revolut**, which allow US residents to open accounts remotely.

---

## Conclusion

**Company registration** in the UK is a powerful move to scale globally. It provides a foothold in Europe and a prestigious legal structure for your brand.
