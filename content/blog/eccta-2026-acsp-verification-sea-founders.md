# ECCTA 2026 and ACSP Verification Explained for Non-Resident SEA Founders

## Complying with New UK Identity Rules

The Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2026 (ECCTA) has introduced significant changes for UK company directors and PSCs. For non-resident founders in Southeast Asia (SEA), these rules are now being strictly enforced.

### 1. Mandatory Identity Verification
Every director and Person with Significant Control (PSC) of a UK Ltd must have their identity verified. This can be done through an **Authorized Corporate Service Provider (ACSP)** like us.

### 2. Fully Digital Verification
For founders in Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam, and Indonesia, the process is now fully digital. You can verify your identity using a digital passport and face-match technology from your home.

### 3. ACSP Advantage
As an ACSP, we handle the entire verification process for you. We provide the necessary identity and address checks required by Companies House.

Compliance is easier than ever with our ACSP-ready formation packages. Avoid delays and ensure your UK Ltd is properly registered from the start.
