# Exporting Indian Fashion & Apparel: Why a London Registered Office is Your Gateway to Europe

Indian fashion, from intricate ethnic wear to sustainable modern apparel, is capturing the imagination of the global market. For designers and manufacturers in Delhi, Mumbai, and Tirupur, the **USA and European markets** offer premium margins. However, selling high-end fashion requires a high-end image.

This is where a **UK based** limited company with a prestigious **registered office address** in London becomes your most valuable asset.

## Building Brand Authority in the West
In the fashion world, perception is reality. When you **open a company in the uk**, your business is no longer just a "foreign supplier." You become a "UK-registered fashion brand." This distinction allows you to:
- Secure space in European showrooms.
- Partner with high-end boutiques that require local invoicing.
- Sell on platforms like Net-a-Porter or Farfetch with a British business identity.

## Strategic SIC Codes for Fashion Exporters
To correctly categorize your **fashion wear** or **textile** business at **Companies House**, you must select the right SIC codes:
-   **14131**: Manufacture of other men's outerwear.
-   **14132**: Manufacture of other women's outerwear.
-   **46420**: Wholesale of clothing and footwear.

These codes are essential when you apply to **open a uk business bank account**, as they prove the legitimacy of your apparel business to banking compliance officers.

### Logistics and VAT Efficiency
Operating as a UK entity allows you to manage VAT registration more effectively. You can hold stock in UK fulfillment centers and ship to customers in the EU with minimal friction, ensuring your **textile** and apparel products reach the customer faster and cheaper.

## Earning for India
By scaling your fashion brand through a UK hub, you generate significant foreign exchange. These funds, when repatriated to India, help fuel your local manufacturing units and contribute to India's position as a global export leader.
