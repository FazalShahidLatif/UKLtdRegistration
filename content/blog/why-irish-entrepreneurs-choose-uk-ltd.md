# Why Irish Entrepreneurs are Choosing UK LTDs over Irish DACs in 2026

While Ireland is a fantastic place to do business, many Irishfounders are choosing to headquarter their digital or service-based businesses in the UK. Why? It often comes down to speed, cost, and the "London Effect".

Here is why a UK LTD is a strategic asset for Irish founders.

## 1. Formation Speed
In the UK, you can have a company incorporated within **24 hours**. In Ireland, the CRO process can often take 5-10 working days, even with the Core system.

## 2. Lower Initial Costs
The UK’s Companies House maintains some of the lowest filing fees in Europe. While the 2026 fee rose to £100, it remains highly competitive compared to many Eurozone registries.

## 3. The Prestige of a London Address
For companies targeting global clients (especially in the US or Asia), a **London address** carries immense weight. It signals stability, heritage, and access to the world's leading financial hub.

## 4. Access to UK Venture Capital
If you are building a tech startup, the UK’s VC ecosystem is roughly 8x larger than Ireland's. Having a UK LTD makes you "funding-ready" for London-based investors who may be hesitant to invest in foreign legal structures.

## 5. Ease of Banking (FinTech Ecosystem)
While Ireland has seen banking exits (like Ulster Bank and KBC), the UK’s FinTech scene is thriving. With a UK LTD, you gain easier access to Tide, Monzo Business, and advanced features in Wise/Revolut Business.

## Is it Right for You?
If you are selling digital services, consulting, or e-commerce products internationally, the UK LTD provides a lean, efficient, and prestigious vehicle that scales faster than almost any other option in Europe.
