# How to Register a UK Company from the USA: Step-by-Step

## The Bridge Across the Atlantic

For US-based entrepreneurs, the UK represents the most logical first step for international expansion. With a shared language, similar legal foundations, and a favorable tax treaty, the UK is a "home away from home" for American business.

However, the logistics of registering a company 3,000 miles away can be daunting. This guide provides the definitive, 2026 step-by-step process for US citizens and residents to incorporate a UK Private Limited Company without leaving their desk.

---

## Step 1: Choose Your Structure (Hint: It’s likely an Ltd)

Most US entrepreneurs choose a **Private Limited Company (Ltd)**. It is the equivalent of a US C-Corp but with the flexibility often associated with an LLC. Unlike the US, where "LLCs" are common, the UK Ltd is the standard for almost all small to medium-sized businesses.

---

## Step 2: The "Registered Office" Challenge

Under UK law, every company must have a physical address in the UK (specifically in the jurisdiction of registration, e.g., England & Wales). You cannot use a US address.

**The Solution**: Use a professional Registered Office service. At UK Ltd Registration, we provide a prestigious London or Edinburgh address that handles your official government mail and keeps your US residential address off the public record.

---

## Step 3: Provide Officer Information

You will need to appoint at least one **Director** and one **Shareholder**.
*   **Can they be the same person?** Yes.
*   **Do they need to be UK residents?** No. US citizens can be 100% owners and directors of a UK company.
*   **Identity Verification**: In 2026, you will need to provide high-quality digital scans of your US Passport and a utility bill for proof of address to comply with UK Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations.

---

## Step 4: Choose Your SIC Code

You must tell the UK government what your company does. Using our **SIC Code Finder**, you can select the correct Standard Industrial Classification code. For many US digital businesses, common codes include:
*   **62012**: Business and domestic software development
*   **73110**: Advertising agencies
*   **47910**: Retail sale via Internet

---

## Step 5: Incorporation and Digital Documents

Once we submit your application, Companies House usually approves it within **24 hours**. You will receive your digital documents via email:
1.  **Certificate of Incorporation**: The "birth certificate" of your company.
2.  **Memorandum and Articles of Association**: The constitution of your business.
3.  **Share Certificates**: Proof of your ownership.

---

## Step 6: Opening a UK Bank Account from the USA

This is the most critical step for US founders. You need an account to accept payments in GBP and pay UK taxes.
*   **Fintech is King**: We recommend using **Wise Business** or **Revolut Business**. They allow US residents to open a UK account remotely using their US-based identity documents.
*   **Traditional Banks**: Generally difficult for non-residents unless you have a physical presence or significant investment in the UK.

---

## Step 7: Managing the Tax Treaty (Form W-8BEN-E)

The UK and US have a robust **Double Taxation Treaty**. This means you generally don't pay tax on the same dollar/pound twice.
*   **UK Side**: Your company pays UK Corporation Tax on its profits.
*   **US Side**: You report your ownership of a foreign corporation on your US tax return (Forms 5471 or 8865).
*   **Expert Tip**: Always consult with a cross-border tax specialist to ensure you are optimizing your US tax position when owning a UK entity.

---

Registering a UK company from the USA is a powerful move for any founder looking to go global. It provides a foothold in Europe, a prestigious legal structure, and access to a massive new customer base.
