# Industrial Lubricants & Gases: Scaling High-Ticket Global Tenders with a UK Entity

Industrial lubricants—both **high and low temperature**—and specialized **industrial gases** are the lifeblood of modern manufacturing, energy, and aerospace sectors. For entrepreneurs in Asian countries, these are "high-ticket" items that frequently appear in lucrative local government and industrial tenders.

By using a **UK based** limited company, you can position yourself as a premium supplier and win these contracts without ever leaving your home country.

## Why Tendering Departments Prefer UK Suppliers
Procurement officers in the energy and military sectors look for stability and quality. A UK Ltd provides:
-   **Certified Compliance**: UK entities are perceived to adhere to higher quality standards, making your bid for **industrial lubricants** or **industrial gases** more competitive.
-   **Transparent Banking**: **Open a uk business bank account** to handle large contract payments. Having a GBP account adds a layer of professionalism that local firms often lack.
-   **Direct Sourcing**: A UK company allows you to negotiate directly with European manufacturers of high-spec lubricants, bypassing local intermediaries and increasing your tender margins.

## Essential SIC Codes for the Energy Sector
When you **form a uk company** for this niche, your **company registration** must be accurate:
-   **46711**: Wholesale of petroleum and petroleum products (**Lubricants**).
-   **46719**: Wholesale of other fuels and related products (**Industrial Gases**).

These codes are essential for your **company details** to be accepted by the compliance departments of large industrial clients and **high street banks**.
