1.  **Local Receiving Accounts:** Payoneer issues real, local bank details in **GBP, USD, EUR, CAD, AUD, and JPY** linked directly under your UK Limited Company name.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Payoneer Business Setup for UK LTD

Getting approved for a corporate Payoneer account in 2026 requires strict compliance and precise documentation. Follow this exact workflow to guarantee a 95%+ approval rate.

### Step 1: Secure Your Corporate Foundation
Before applying to Payoneer, your UK Limited Company must be fully active at Companies House. Payoneer will query the public register dynamically.
*   **Target Status:** Ensure your company has active SIC codes (Standard Industrial Classification) that accurately reflect your model (e.g., `47910` for retail sale via mail order houses or internet).
*   **Virtual Headquarters:** You must use an appropriate, physical UK Registered Office Address (PO boxes are legally prohibited under 2026 ECCTA regulations).

### Step 2: Prepare Your KYC Information Package
Payoneer operates under strict global AML (Anti-Money Laundering) parameters. You must collect the following documents *before* initiating the application:

*   **For the Company:**
    *   UK Certificate of Incorporation (PDF).
    *   Companies House Current Information Report (detailing active directors and PSCs).
    *   Proof of UK business address (such as a utility bill, lease agreement, or official HMRC tax correspondence matching your registered office).
*   **For the Directors & Owners (PSCs holding 25%+):**
    *   High-resolution biometric passport (Driver’s licenses are generally rejected for non-resident checks).
    *   Personal proof of residency in your home country (Bank statement or utility bill dated within the last 90 days matching your passport name).

### Step 3: Initiate the "Company" Registration Path
Navigate to the Payoneer portal and select the **"Company"** track. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Avoid the Individual Trap:** Do *not* register as an "Individual" or "Sole Trader". Your account must be opened under the legal structure of your **UK Limited Company**. Registering incorrectly will lead to immediate Stripe linkage failures and bank rejection.

1.  **Legal Entity Name:** Enter your UK LTD name *exactly* as it appears on your Certificate of Incorporation (including the spaces and the word "LIMITED").
2.  **Registration Number:** Provide the 8-digit UK Company Registration Number (CRN) issued by Companies House.

### Step 4: Describe Your Business Model (The Approval Maker)
Payoneer manually audits how your business generates revenue. To pass this compliance check:
*   Provide a functional, professional business website or active store link (e.g. Shopify, Amazon Seller, or agency landing page).
*   Your website must clearly detail your services, products, pricing, and contact information.
*   **Avoid High-Risk Keywords:** Never mention terms like *crypto, gambling, financial consulting, or unlicensed investments* in your business description, as these trigger instant rejection.

## 💳 Linking Payoneer to Stripe UK

One of the biggest reasons non-residents open a UK Limited Company is to access a **Stripe UK payment gateway**. Payoneer makes this incredibly simple.

### The Standard Mapping Process:
1.  Log in to your **Payoneer Account** and navigate to **Get Paid** → **Receiving Accounts**.
2.  Select your **USD or GBP receiving account** details (Sort Code, Account Number, and Bank Name - typically Citibank or Barclays).
3.  Log in to your **Stripe UK Dashboard**.
4.  Navigate to **Settings** → **External Bank Accounts**.
5.  Enter your Payoneer GBP details.
6.  *Payout Harmony:* Stripe will process your customers' credit card payments in GBP, hold them, and pay them out daily directly to your Payoneer account for free.

## ⚖️ Payoneer vs Wise Business for E-Commerce

For dropshippers and Amazon FBA sellers, choosing between Payoneer and Wise depends heavily on your geographic location:

*   **Wise Business** is superior for **global currency conversions** and low-fee cross-border payments. It provides the absolute cheapest FX rates (typically ~0.4%) and is ideal if you need to pay virtual assistants or suppliers in different currencies.
*   **Payoneer Business** is superior for **high-volume marketplace integrations** and localized payouts in emerging markets. If you reside in Pakistan, Bangladesh, or the Philippines, Payoneer's integration with local banking networks (such as JazzCash or local commercial banks) provides much higher transaction reliability and faster local settlement.
