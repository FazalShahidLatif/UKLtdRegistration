## SIC Codes for the South Asian Trader
Choosing the correct **SIC code** is vital for your **company registration**. Common codes include:
-   **46410**: Wholesale of **textiles**.
-   **46420**: Wholesale of clothing and footwear.
-   **46370**: Wholesale of coffee, tea, cocoa and **spices**.
