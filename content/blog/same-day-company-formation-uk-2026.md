## Standard vs. Same Day Formation Processing

When you register a UK Limited Company, the application goes to Companies House for approval. By default, applications enter a standard queue. 

**Standard Processing:** Usually takes 24 to 48 working hours. In peak periods, this can stretch to 3-5 days.
**Same Day Processing (Fast-Track):** Jumps to the front of the queue. If submitted before the strict afternoon deadline, Companies House guarantees processing the application on the self-same working day (often within 3-4 hours).

## The Golden Rules for Same Day Success

A Same-Day application is not magic; it requires exact timing and total accuracy. If your application contains errors, Companies House will reject it, and you'll lose the same-day guarantee. 

Here’s how to ensure success:

### 1. The Strict Cut-Off Deadline (3:00 PM UK Time)
To guarantee processing today, Companies House must receive the digital application **before 3:00 PM (GMT/BST)** on a normal working day (Monday to Friday, excluding Bank Holidays). 

*If submitted at 3:05 PM on a Tuesday, your company will be formed first thing Wednesday morning.* 

### 2. Flawless Details 
A single typo in a director's birth date or an invalid postal code will trigger a rejection. Because same-day submissions are expedited, the rejection happens rapidly. Our team pre-screens all same-day applications to correct these fatal errors before submission.

### 3. Choosing a "Safe" Name
If your proposed company name contains a "Sensitive Word" (e.g., "Bank", "Trust", "Group", "International", or words implying royal connections), it triggers an extended manual review. **Same-day processing is rarely possible for sensitive names.** 
Stick to distinct, standard business names to glide smoothly through the automated checks.
