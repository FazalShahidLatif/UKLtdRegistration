# Getting Paid in GBP, USD and EUR from Southeast Asia using a UK Ltd

## Solving the Global Income Challenge

For founders in Southeast Asia (SEA), collecting payments in Western currencies can be a major hurdle. Local banks often have high FX fees and slow processing times.

### 1. The UK Ltd Gateway
By using a UK Ltd, you unlock access to the UK's advanced banking and payment infrastructure. This allows you to open accounts with local sort codes and GBP/USD account numbers.

### 2. Stripe and Wise Integration
The most common setup for SEA founders:
- **Stripe UK**: Accept card payments globally in USD, EUR, and GBP.
- **Wise Business**: A multi-currency account to hold and transfer funds at mid-market rates.

### 3. Minimal Friction
This setup ensures your income flows smoothly from global clients to your businesses with minimal fees and delays.

### 4. Summary
Getting paid globally from Southeast Asia is easier than ever with a UK Ltd and a modern digital banking stack.

> [!NOTE]
> Ensure your UK Ltd is properly registered and your identity is verified through an ACSP to smooth the account opening process.
